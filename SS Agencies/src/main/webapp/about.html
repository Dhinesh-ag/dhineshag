About SS Agencies - Authorized Amul Ice Cream Dealer

SS Agencies is a leading distributor of Amul ice cream, serving the Delta regions with a wide range of premium quality ice cream products since 2010. Our mission is to bring the delightful flavors and rich tradition of Amul to every corner of Delta, ensuring our customers experience the highest satisfaction with every scoop.

Our Commitment to Quality

At SS Agencies, we prioritize quality in every aspect of our operations. We work closely with Amul to maintain the highest standards of freshness, taste, and hygiene in our storage and distribution processes. Each Amul ice cream product we deliver undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure it meets our stringent criteria before reaching our customers.

Our Product Range

We offer a diverse selection of Amul ice cream flavors to cater to the varied preferences of our customers. From classic favorites like Vanilla and Chocolate to exotic delights such as Mango Tango and Almond Crunch, our product range is designed to delight ice cream lovers of all ages.

Customer Satisfaction

At the heart of SS Agencies is a commitment to customer satisfaction. We strive to create memorable experiences for our customers by providing prompt service, reliable delivery, and personalized support. Our dedicated team is passionate about ensuring every interaction with SS Agencies exceeds expectations.

Community and Values

SS Agencies is deeply rooted in the communities we serve. We believe in fostering strong relationships with our customers, partners, and stakeholders based on transparency, integrity, and mutual respect. Our values guide us in our daily operations and inspire us to continuously improve and innovate.
