About Us

Welcome to Amul Ice Cream Parlour, where we serve the best ice cream flavors...

Amul Ice Cream Parlour has been delighting customers with its wide range of delicious ice cream flavors since 2001. Located in the heart of the city, we pride ourselves on using the finest ingredients to create creamy and flavorful ice creams that satisfy every palate.

Our journey began with a simple goal: to provide families and friends with a place to enjoy quality ice cream in a welcoming environment. Over the years, we have expanded our menu to include classic flavors like Vanilla and Chocolate, as well as innovative creations such as Mango Tango and Nutty Caramel.

At Amul Ice Cream Parlour, customer satisfaction is our top priority. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply craving a sweet treat, our friendly staff is dedicated to ensuring you have a memorable experience every time you visit.

Join us at Amul Ice Cream Parlour and indulge in the joy of creamy, flavorful ice creams that are made with love and served with a smile. We look forward to welcoming you!
